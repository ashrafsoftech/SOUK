SLIDERS

SLIDE 1

The vertebra column, also known as the Backbone or Spinal column, is the central supporting structure of the skeleton.
The vertebra column forms the backbone of vertebrate animals and protect the spinal cord.It is made up of five groups
of bones called the vertebrae (singular: vertebra) in mammals. In mammals. In human, it consist of 33 vertebrae, in it
consist of 46 vertebrae. The vertebrae are held one to the others with strong ligaments having compressible cartilage pads
intervertebral discs between consecutive vertebrae.
