About Abuja Ubarn Mass Transport Limited

Abuja Urban Mass Transport Company Limited (AUMTCO) was established by the then Ministry of
Federal Capital Territory now the Federal Capital Territory Administration (FCTA) in 1984 as Abuja
Bus Service (ABS) and later registered on 13th November, 1989 as Abuja Urban Mass Transport
Company Limited, under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 1990. We are part of the Abuja
Investments Company Limited; Group. We operate a comprehensive Intra City Bus Service in the
Federal Capital Territory from a purpose built Head Office on Plot 185 Cadastral Zone F03 (ONEX),
Usuma District Kubwa Express Way, P.M.B. 168, Abuja, Nigeria and Depots in other parts of the
Federal Capital Territory.

OUR SERVICES

One of our core values is the provision of efficient, effective and affordable urban public service delivery in the FCT without compromising comfort and safety.
